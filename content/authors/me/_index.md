## About Me

I am an undergraduate aerospace engineering researcher at the University of Cincinnati, working with **Prof. Prashant Khare** and **Dr. Kamin Manu** in the Computational Multiphysics Laboratory. My research lives at the intersection of high-speed compressible flows, multiphase dynamics, combustion, and machine-learning-accelerated CFD — from shock-driven droplet fragmentation and gel propellant atomization to LES of recirculation structures and spray combustion in high-speed combustors. I'm preparing to pursue a PhD in this area and continue building computational tools that bridge first-principles physics and modern machine learning.
