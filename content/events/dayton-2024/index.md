abstract: |
  We present the first numerical study of how cavities embedded inside liquid droplets alter the
  fragmentation behavior of those droplets under shock loading in supersonic flows. Schlieren-style
  visualizations, baroclinic torque distributions, and pressure-field analyses reveal a fragmentation
  pathway distinct from the homogeneous-droplet case.
