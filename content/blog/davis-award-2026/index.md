I'm honored to have received the **Davis Professor R.T. Award for the Best Undergraduate Student in Computational Research** at the University of Cincinnati. Huge thanks to my advisors **Dr. Prashant Khare** and **Dr. Kamin Manu**, and to my collaborators across the Muliphase Flow & Combustion Laboratory.

## About the award

The **Professor R.T. Davis Endowment** honors the late R.T. Davis, who chaired UC's Aerospace Engineering department, and supports students with a preference for the field of *computational mechanics* — fitting for the recognition I received in computational research.

**Learn more about Professor R.T. Davis and the endowment:**

- [Tom and Gloria Davis honor parents through UC scholarships](https://www.uc.edu/news/articles/2022/07/n21101219.html) — UC News, July 2022. The story of how Tom and Gloria Davis established the endowment in honor of Tom's parents.
- [UC Aerospace Engineering and Engineering Mechanics department awards 40 scholarships](https://www.uc.edu/news/articles/2019/05/n20834009.html) — UC News, May 2019. Context on the broader scholarship program, including the R.T. Davis Scholarship.
