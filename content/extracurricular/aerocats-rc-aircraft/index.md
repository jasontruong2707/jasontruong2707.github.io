## What I did

As **Manufacturing & Testing Engineer** with AeroCats (UC's RC Aircraft Design & Manufacturing Club):

- **Constructed and assembled 4 RC aircraft and UAVs with 16-foot wingspans** for national intercollegiate competitions, using laser-cutting techniques and over 1,000 pieces of hardwood, pine, and balsa.
- **Conducted thrust testing on 6 turbine models** of varying lengths via 3 distinct testing methods, evaluating aircraft electrical engine performance.
- **Analyzed thrust force data** across turbines to identify the optimal design and computed maximum payload capacity for flight readiness.

## Outcome

**4th overall in the Regular Class Competition at SAE Aero Design West, May 2023.**
